About Us

The LSPU-LBC Training Tracker System is a project developed by a team of passionate and innovative Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) students from the College of Computer Studies, Laguna State Polytechnic University – Los Baños Campus. This system was designed to streamline the documentation and monitoring of training programs for all teaching and non-teaching personnel, supporting continuous professional development and ensuring timely compliance with quarterly training requirements.
